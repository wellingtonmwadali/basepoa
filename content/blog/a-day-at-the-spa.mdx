# Wellness in the Hills: Serenity Spa

There is something inherently healing about the highland air. The crispness, the quiet, the scent of eucalyptus—it all sets the stage for deep relaxation. But if you want to take that relaxation to the next level, a visit to **Serenity Spa** is mandatory.

## The Setting
Located inside a refurbished colonial stone manor, Serenity Spa feels miles away from civilization. The treatment rooms open up to private gardens filled with ancient trees and singing birds. There is no artificial spa music here; the soundtrack is entirely natural.

## The Treatments
Serenity specializes in treatments that utilize local highland ingredients.

*   **The Signature Tea Scrub:** Using crushed, locally grown tea leaves and organic oils, this full-body scrub is invigorating and leaves your skin incredibly soft.
*   **The Deep Tissue Highland Massage:** Perfect for those who have spent the morning hiking or e-biking. The therapists use warm, locally sourced macadamia oil.
*   **The Eucalyptus Steam:** Before your treatment, spend 15 minutes in the steam room infused with fresh eucalyptus harvested right from the property.

## The Verdict
If you are visiting Tigoni for a romantic escape or a girls' weekend, book your treatments at least three weeks in advance. The spa is intimate and spots fill up quickly. 

Plan to stay an extra hour after your treatment to sip herbal tea on the manor’s veranda. It is pure bliss.
