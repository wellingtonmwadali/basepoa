# Top 5 Photography Spots in Tigoni

Tigoni is undeniably one of the most photogenic regions in Kenya. Whether you are shooting on a professional DSLR or just your iPhone, the highland light and dramatic landscapes guarantee incredible shots. 

Here are the 5 spots you cannot miss:

### 1. The Aberdare Viewpoint
**Best time to shoot:** 6:30 AM.
If you arrive just as the sun is coming up, the mist sits in the valleys while the peaks of the Aberdares catch the golden morning light. On a clear day, use a telephoto lens to capture Mount Kenya in the distance.

### 2. The Siri Cabin A-Frame
**Best time to shoot:** Dusk (6:00 PM).
The iconic A-frame cabin glowing warmly from within against the darkening tea fields is a quintessential Tigoni shot. The symmetry of the architecture against the organic lines of the farm is striking.

### 3. Deep in the Tea Fields
**Best time to shoot:** Mid-morning (9:00 AM).
Join a Farm Tour to get up close. The contrast between the bright green tea leaves and the deep red volcanic soil makes for incredible color photography. 

### 4. Tigoni Dam
**Best time to shoot:** Late afternoon (4:30 PM).
The calm waters of the dam act like a mirror for the surrounding acacia trees and sky. It’s a fantastic spot for long-exposure photography if you bring a tripod.

### 5. Limuru Heights Bar & Grill
**Best time to shoot:** Sunset (6:30 PM).
Grab a cocktail and head to the edge of the terrace. The view over the Rift Valley as the sun dips below the horizon is unmatched. Silhouettes work beautifully here.
