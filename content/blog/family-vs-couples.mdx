# Tigoni Travel Styles

Planning a trip to Tigoni can feel overwhelming because there is so much to do. But the secret to the perfect highland trip is tailoring the itinerary to your specific travel group. 

Here is our breakdown of how to experience Tigoni, depending on who you are traveling with.

## Travel Style 1: The Family Adventure

When traveling with kids or large family groups, you need activities that are engaging but not physically exhausting, and accommodations with plenty of space.

### Best Activities for Families
*   **Twin Rivers Picnic:** A highly accessible area where kids can run free in a safe, enclosed valley while adults set up a picnic by the river.
*   **Farm Tours:** Unlike the strenuous hikes, farm walks are gentle. Kids love seeing how tea is made and interacting with farm animals.
*   **Brown's Cheese Tour:** This is highly interactive and ends with a meal, making it perfect for keeping younger travelers entertained.

### Where to Stay
*   **Siri Mitini:** This sprawling vacation home sleeps 10 and has a massive private garden, making it the ultimate family basecamp.

### Where to Eat
*   **Muna Tree Café:** The large outdoor lawns give children space to play while you wait for your wood-fired pizzas.

---

## Travel Style 2: The Romantic Escape

If you are celebrating an anniversary or just want a quiet weekend away from the city, Tigoni offers incredible seclusion and luxury.

### Best Activities for Couples
*   **Serenity Spa:** Book a couple's massage in the colonial stone manor, followed by a private herbal wrap session.
*   **E-Bike Ridge Trail:** A quiet, scenic activity that allows you to explore the stunning landscapes at your own pace without the exhaustion of a major hike.
*   **Aberdare Viewpoint at Sunset:** Pack a thermos of hot tea (or something stronger) and drive up to the viewpoint for a private, unforgettable sunset.

### Where to Stay
*   **Siri Cabin:** The iconic A-frame cabin is built for two. It’s completely secluded in the tea fields and features a stunning glass front that lets you watch the stars from bed.

### Where to Eat
*   **Limuru Heights Bar & Grill:** Reserve a corner table for an intimate dinner with panoramic views over the Rift Valley. 
*   **Kentmere Club:** For a touch of historic grandeur, enjoy fine dining in their colonial-era ballroom.
