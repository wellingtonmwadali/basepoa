# The Ultimate Tigoni Weekend

Tigoni is only a short drive from Nairobi, but it feels like stepping into another world. The emerald tea fields, misty mornings, and crisp highland air make it the perfect weekend escape. But with so much to do—from farm tours to artisan cheese tasting—how do you fit it all into 48 hours?

Here is our ultimate, step-by-step weekend itinerary.

## Day 1: Adventure & Artisan Flavors

### Morning (8:00 AM – 11:30 AM): The E-Bike Ridge Trail
Start your weekend with some adrenaline. The Tigoni ridge trail is best experienced in the early morning before the mist fully lifts.
*   **Time Commitment:** 3 to 3.5 hours.
*   **What to expect:** You'll cover roughly 15km of farm trails. E-bikes make the hills effortless, allowing you to focus on the spectacular views of the Great Rift Valley.
*   **Pro Tip:** Wear layers! It’s cold at 8 AM but you’ll warm up quickly once the sun breaks through.

### Lunch (12:30 PM – 2:30 PM): Brown's Cheese Factory
After your ride, head over to Brown’s Cheese for a specialized tasting experience.
*   **Time Commitment:** 2 hours.
*   **The Vibe:** Relaxed, educational, and delicious. You'll get a tour of the dairy followed by a sprawling tasting board on the lawn. 

### Afternoon (3:00 PM – 5:00 PM): Check-in and Unwind
Head to your accommodation (we highly recommend **Thayu House** or **Siri Cabin** if you want something secluded). Spend the late afternoon reading on the porch with a hot cup of highland tea.

### Dinner (7:00 PM onwards): Limuru Heights Bar & Grill
Wrap up your first day at Limuru Heights. Perched at 2,300 meters, it offers the most dramatic sunset views in the region. Order the slow-cooked brisket and enjoy the fire pits.

---

## Day 2: Heritage & Relaxation

### Morning (9:00 AM – 1:00 PM): The Tea Farm Experience
You can't visit Tigoni without diving deep into its most famous export: tea.
*   **Time Commitment:** 4 hours.
*   **The Breakdown:** A proper tea tour is split into three sub-activities: 
    1. **The Field Walk (1 hr):** Learn the history of the estates and how to pluck the perfect "two leaves and a bud."
    2. **The Factory (1.5 hrs):** See the processing, withering, and drying phases.
    3. **The Tasting (1.5 hrs):** A sommelier-style tasting of different grades of black, green, and purple teas.

### Lunch (1:30 PM – 3:30 PM): Muna Tree Café
For a laid-back Sunday lunch, Muna Tree Café is the local favorite. Grab a table under the giant fig tree and order their famous wood-fired pizzas.

### Late Afternoon (4:00 PM – 6:00 PM): Serenity Spa
Before driving back down to the city, book a 60-minute highland herbal massage at Serenity Spa. It’s the perfect way to reset before returning to reality.
