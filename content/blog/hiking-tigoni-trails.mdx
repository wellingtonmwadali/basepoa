# Tigoni Hiking Guide

Tigoni’s landscape is incredibly diverse. Within a 10-kilometer radius, you can transition from perfectly manicured farm trails into dense, wild indigenous forests. 

Whether you want a casual post-lunch stroll or a serious cardio workout, here are the best hiking options in the highlands.

## Level 1: The Gentle Stroll
**Where:** The Tea Estate Trails
**Duration:** 1 to 2 hours
**Vibe:** Relaxing, scenic, and flat.
If you aren't looking to break a sweat, the dirt farm roads that weave through the tea estates are perfect. There is very little elevation gain, making this ideal for young families or older travelers. You’ll walk past tea pickers and enjoy endless views of the green ridges.

## Level 2: The Forest Walk
**Where:** Tigoni Forest Reserve
**Duration:** 2 to 3 hours
**Vibe:** Shaded, immersive nature.
The forest reserve offers well-maintained trails under the canopy of indigenous trees. It’s significantly cooler in the forest, so bring a light jacket. This trail involves some moderate elevation changes and is fantastic for bird watchers.

## Level 3: The Escarpment Challenge
**Where:** The Great Rift Valley Escarpment Trail
**Duration:** 4 to 6 hours
**Vibe:** Strenuous, rewarding, and steep.
This is for serious hikers. You’ll start in the highlands and hike down toward the floor of the Great Rift Valley, before turning around and making the grueling climb back up. The views are unparalleled, but you need proper hiking boots, plenty of water, and a good level of fitness.
