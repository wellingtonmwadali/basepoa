# Packing for the Highlands

The golden rule of Tigoni is **layers**. Because of the high altitude, the weather can turn on a dime. You might find yourself sweating on a hike at noon, only to be shivering by the fireplace at dinner.

Here is your definitive packing list for a weekend in the highlands.

## The Essentials

*   **A Warm Fleece or Sweater:** Do not underestimate the evening chill. As soon as the sun drops behind the escarpment, the temperature plummets. 
*   **A Light Rain Jacket:** Even in the dry season, the highlands are prone to sudden, 15-minute misty showers. A packable windbreaker/rain shell is mandatory.
*   **Sturdy Walking Shoes:** Whether you plan to hike or just walk from your cabin to the restaurant, the ground in Tigoni is often uneven and damp. Leave the heels in Nairobi.
*   **Sunscreen:** Because the air is cool, you won't realize how strong the sun is. At 2,200 meters, the UV index is very high.

## The Nice-to-Haves

*   **Binoculars:** The birdlife in the indigenous forest patches is spectacular.
*   **A Good Book:** There is very little nightlife in Tigoni. The evenings are designed for reading by the fire with a glass of wine or a cup of hot tea.
*   **A Daypack:** If you are e-biking or hiking, a small backpack to hold your water bottle and your shed layers is incredibly helpful.

Pack smart, and you'll be able to comfortably enjoy everything the highlands have to offer, rain or shine!
