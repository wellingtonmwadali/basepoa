# A Guide to the Highland Seasons

Because Tigoni sits at an altitude of over 2,200 meters (7,200 feet), it boasts a micro-climate quite different from nearby Nairobi or the Rift Valley floor. 

While you can visit year-round, the "vibe" of your trip will change drastically depending on the month. Here is what you need to know.

## The Clear Season (January – March)
If you want to see for miles, this is the time to come.
*   **The Weather:** Dry, warm days (up to 25°C) and cool, crisp nights (around 12°C). 
*   **The Highlights:** This is the absolute best time for **hiking** and **e-biking**. The trails are dry, and the skies are incredibly clear.
*   **The Views:** From the Aberdare Viewpoint, you have a high chance of seeing both Mount Kenya and Mount Kilimanjaro on the same morning.

## The Long Rains (April – May)
This is Tigoni’s quietest season, but for romantic getaways, it’s arguably the best.
*   **The Weather:** Heavy afternoon showers, very cold nights, and misty mornings that hang low over the tea fields.
*   **The Highlights:** The tea fields turn a vibrant, almost neon shade of emerald green. It’s the perfect time to book a cozy cabin like **Siri Mitini**, light the fireplace, and enjoy **spa treatments** or long lunches at **Kentmere Club**.
*   **What to Pack:** Serious rain gear, waterproof boots, and your favorite books.

## The Cool Season (June – August)
Often referred to as the "European Summer" of Kenya.
*   **The Weather:** Overcast, cool, and dry. Temperatures rarely rise above 18°C during the day and can drop into the single digits at night.
*   **The Highlights:** This is the prime season for **Tea Tours**. The cool weather makes walking through the estates incredibly pleasant, and hot tea tastings feel entirely appropriate. 

## The Short Rains (October – November)
A beautiful transition season.
*   **The Weather:** Sunny mornings followed by short, dramatic thunderstorms in the late afternoon.
*   **The Highlights:** The sunsets during this season are spectacular. The combination of breaking storm clouds and the setting sun creates incredible photography opportunities. It’s the best season for late lunches at **Limuru Heights Bar & Grill**.

### Summary
*   **For Outdoor Adventurers:** January to March.
*   **For Cozy Cabin Retreats:** April and May.
*   **For Photographers:** October and November.
